Hi! I am a PhD candidate at Imperial College London Computing under the supervision of Prof. Emil Lupu. My research primarily focuses on AI security and Trustworthy AI, specifically in the areas of adversarial attacks and defenses. I am also interested in Computer Vision models, Large Language Models (LLMs), and Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs).

I am currently part of the Safe and Trusted AI PhD programme, a joint initiative with King's College London. Through my research, I aim to develop robust and reliable AI systems that can withstand adversarial threats while maintaining high performance in real-world applications.
